Eugene & Lane County
Neighborhoods

Explore 20 distinct neighborhoods across Eugene, Springfield, and the surrounding Lane County communities. Each area offers a unique character, price point, and investment profile. Whether you are buying your first home, relocating, or building an investment portfolio, understanding the neighborhoods is essential.

Eugene
Eugene Neighborhoods

South Eugene

Median: $565,000

Eugene's premier residential neighborhood with sweeping Cascade views, top-rated schools, and proximity to Spencer Butte hiking trails. The luxury epicenter of Lane County.

Luxury Family Nature

Whiteaker

Median: $385,000

Eugene's most creative and walkable neighborhood, home to craft breweries, restaurants, and a vibrant arts scene. Rapid appreciation and strong rental demand.

Urban Walkable Trending

Fairmount

Median: $525,000

Historic hilltop neighborhood near the University of Oregon with Craftsman homes, Hendricks Park, and walkable access to campus and South Willamette dining.

Historic Walkable UO Adjacent

College Hill

Median: $475,000

Tree-lined streets, classic architecture, and proximity to the University of Oregon and downtown. A blend of historic character and urban convenience.

Historic UO Adjacent Urban

River Road / Santa Clara

Median: $395,000

Larger lots, a suburban feel, and easy access to downtown Eugene. Popular with families and rental investors seeking space and value in the metro core.

Family Value Larger Lots

Bethel-Danebo

Median: $355,000

West Eugene's most active market for fix-and-flip investors. Strong fundamentals, improving amenities, and the best acquisition-to-ARV spreads in the metro area.

Investment Fix & Flip Value

Southwest Hills

Median: $535,000

Private, wooded estates on multi-acre parcels with territorial views. Minutes from downtown but a world away in character. Eugene's most secluded luxury enclave.

Luxury Privacy Acreage

Churchill

Median: $365,000

Affordable south Eugene with good freeway access and proximity to Lane Community College. A solid market for first-time buyers and value-oriented investors.

Affordable Commuter Investment

Cal Young

Median: $450,000

Established north Eugene neighborhood with well-maintained mid-century homes, excellent schools, and a quiet residential character. Popular with growing families.

Family Schools Established

Friendly

Median: $420,000

Charming neighborhood along Friendly Street with local shops, cafes, and a strong sense of community. Close to Amazon Park and well-connected to downtown.

Walkable Community Central

Downtown Eugene

Median: $390,000

The urban core with condos, lofts, and mixed-use developments. Walkable to Hult Center, Saturday Market, and the Willamette riverfront. Growing density and amenities.

Urban Walkable Condos

Harlow

Median: $380,000

Northeast Eugene neighborhood with good access to I-105 and Gateway Mall. A mix of single-family homes and multi-family properties at competitive price points.

Commuter Multi-Family Value
Springfield
Springfield Neighborhoods

Gateway / Springfield

Median: $365,000

Lane County's top emerging market. Transit investments, new commercial development, and affordable residential prices are driving rapid transformation and appreciation.

Emerging Transit Investment

Thurston

Median: $385,000

East Springfield's family-oriented community with newer subdivisions, larger lots, and proximity to the McKenzie River. Strong schools and a suburban lifestyle.

Family Suburban Newer Homes

Downtown Springfield

Median: $340,000

Historic Main Street revitalization with new restaurants, breweries, and mixed-use projects. Affordable entry points and strong upside potential for investors.

Revitalizing Affordable Upside

Mohawk

Median: $355,000

North Springfield neighborhood with a mix of established and newer homes. Good school access, community parks, and competitive pricing for the metro area.

Residential Value Community
Surrounding Communities
Lane County Communities

Cottage Grove

Median: $310,000

Small-town charm 20 miles south of Eugene. Historic covered bridges, Row River Trail, and the lowest entry points in Lane County. Growing investor interest.

Affordable Small Town Investment

Junction City

Median: $345,000

Agricultural community 15 minutes north of Eugene with a growing residential base. Scandinavian heritage, annual festivals, and affordable family housing.

Rural Affordable Growing

Veneta / Fern Ridge

Median: $335,000

West Lane County community near Fern Ridge Reservoir. Rural character, outdoor recreation, and affordable prices attract buyers seeking space and nature access.

Rural Recreation Affordable

Coburg

Median: $475,000

Charming historic village just north of Eugene with antique shops, preserved architecture, and a tight-knit community. Premium pricing for the small-town experience.

Historic Charming Premium
